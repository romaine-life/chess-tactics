# Unit Concepts

## Pawn Direction Lock

Accepted preview: `pawn-shield-south-concept.png`

Direction:
- Normal chess piece first, squad unit second.
- Pawn keeps a compact classic pawn silhouette.
- A small physical shield sits in front of the pawn to make facing direction obvious.
- No face, arms, legs, weapon, or character body.
- No separate pedestal, plinth, trophy base, or oversized base ring as a design feature.
- Style target is pixel-tactics: Into the Breach / Advance Wars clarity with Chessmaster identity.

Open polish pass:
- Make the sprite more pixel-native and less glossy.
- Reduce ornament if it starts reading too royal or ceremonial.
- Keep the shield readable without turning it into a full character pose.
