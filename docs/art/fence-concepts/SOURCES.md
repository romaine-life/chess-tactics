# Fence art provenance

Fence geometry and endpoint topology remain deterministic code. Every visible
material pixel, source image, candidate frame, and proof is a private live-media
record. Typed fence slots declare rail direction, post role, frame geometry,
anchor, material family, and native-size evidence.

The former calibration bridge, static review manifests, copied candidate PNGs,
hash pins, and `build-fence-tiles.py` publisher were retired by ADR-0085. They
must not be restored as fallback or production authority. New candidates are
uploaded from a temporary workspace, mounted on the real editable board, and
accepted only through the backend transaction after owner review.
