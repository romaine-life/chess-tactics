## Acceptance Notes

This batch is accepted as the fourth artwork package, not yet wired into the
game. It captures a distinct thematic pressure from the earlier packages:

- nature and climate as the pressure, not human violence
- summer behaving incorrectly after the Tambora climate shock
- survival as practical adaptation rather than victory
- rural beauty, frost, covered crops, storage, roads, animals, and household
  work
- a positive contrast of people solving a great environmental problem as well as
  they could

Future implementation should treat these as source/reference assets until a
real background-selection pipeline exists.
