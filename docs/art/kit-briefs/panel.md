# Historical kit brief — panel

This locator remains for ADR-0011 references. The old concept-extraction and
repository-output instructions are retired and must not be followed.

Panel geometry and nine-slice regions are code-owned configuration. Source
atoms are fetched from private backend versions into an OS temporary workspace;
the composed panel is uploaded as a candidate for its semantic live-media slot.
Owner review and backend acceptance are the only production transition. No
panel pixels, candidate roster, or accepted pointer belongs in Git.

See `docs/ui-kit-standard.md`, `docs/runtime-asset-contract.md`, and ADR-0081.
